## 1. Definition

A **complex number** is a number of the form  
\[
z = a + ib
\]
where  
- \(a\) is the **real part**,  
- \(b\) is the **imaginary part**,  
- \(i = \sqrt{-1}\).

It is denoted as  
\[
\Re(z) = a,\quad \Im(z) = b
\]

---

## 2. Tamil Meaning (தமிழ் விளக்கம்)

**சிக்கலெண் (Complex Number)** என்பது  
\[
z = a + ib
\]
என்ற வடிவில் எழுதப்படும் எண் ஆகும்.

இங்கு,  
- \(a\) → **மெய்பகுதி (Real Part)**  
- \(b\) → **கற்பனை பகுதி (Imaginary Part)**  
- \(i = \sqrt{-1}\)

---

## 3. Examples for Definition

1. \(z = 3 + 4i\)  
   - Real part = 3  
   - Imaginary part = 4  

2. \(z = -2 + i\)

3. \(z = 5\) (pure real number)

4. \(z = 7i\) (pure imaginary number)

---

## 4. Algebraic Operations on Complex Numbers

### (a) Addition
\[
(a+ib) + (c+id) = (a+c) + i(b+d)
\]

**Example:**  
\[
(2+3i) + (1+4i) = 3 + 7i
\]

---

### (b) Subtraction
\[
(a+ib) - (c+id) = (a-c) + i(b-d)
\]

---

### (c) Multiplication
\[
(a+ib)(c+id) = (ac - bd) + i(ad + bc)
\]

**Example:**  
\[
(1+2i)(3+4i) = -5 + 10i
\]

---

### (d) Division
\[
\frac{a+ib}{c+id} = \frac{(a+ib)(c-id)}{c^2+d^2}
\]

**Example:**  
\[
\frac{1+i}{2-i} = \frac{(1+i)(2+i)}{5}
\]

---

## 5. Important Results and Formulae

1. \(i^2 = -1\)
2. \(i^3 = -i\)
3. \(i^4 = 1\)
4. \(\bar{z} = a - ib\) (Conjugate)
5. \(z\bar{z} = a^2 + b^2 = |z|^2\)

---

## 6. Modulus of a Complex Number

### Definition
\[
|z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}
\]

### Tamil Meaning
ஒரு சிக்கலெண்ணின் **அளவு (Modulus)** என்பது அதன் தூரத்தை குறிக்கும்.

### Example
\[
|3+4i| = 5
\]

---

## 7. Properties of Modulus

1. \(|z| \ge 0\)
2. \(|z_1z_2| = |z_1||z_2|\)
3. \(|\frac{z_1}{z_2}| = \frac{|z_1|}{|z_2|}\)

---

## 8. Theorem

### Triangle Inequality
\[
|z_1 + z_2| \le |z_1| + |z_2|
\]

---

## 9. Proof (Outline)

Let  
\[
z_1 = a+ib,\quad z_2 = c+id
\]

Using modulus properties and square comparison,  
\[
|z_1 + z_2|^2 \le (|z_1| + |z_2|)^2
\]

Hence proved.
